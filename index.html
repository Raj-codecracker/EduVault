How EduVault Works

Simple, secure, and streamlined process for managing student records

1

Institution Registration

Higher Education Institutions register on the platform and set up their organizational profile with necessary credentials and verification.

2

Student Onboarding

Students create accounts with unique digital IDs (APAAR/ABC compatible), linking their academic and personal information securely.

3

Activity Recording

Institutions upload and verify student activities including academic performance, certifications, sports, volunteering, and research.

4

Credit Management

System automatically calculates and maintains credit scores based on NEP 2020 guidelines, enabling seamless credit transfers.

5

Verification & Access

Authorized parties (employers, other institutions) can instantly verify student credentials with permission-based access control.
